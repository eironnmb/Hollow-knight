Why I Love Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight moves me with its atmosphere and interconnected world. Every room and stage feels alive, telling its own story through art and music. The game challenges me with difficult boss fights, hard platforming, and challenge rooms, but it rewards patience and exploration, and each discovery feels amazing. It’s not just about beating bosses, it’s about exploring Hallownest at my own pace, and that sense of accomplishment keeps making me come back.
