Essential Areas

Dirtmouth

The quiet town at the entrance of Hallownest. It serves as the main hub where the Knight returns to after exploring.

City of Tears

A vast, rain-soaked city deep underground. It was once the thriving capital of Hallownest.

Greenpath

A lush, overgrown area filled with vegetation and mysterious creatures. It's the first major region after Dirtmouth.

Fog canyon

Fog Canyon is a glowing cavern filled with jellyfish-like creatures and crackling electricity. Its calm beauty hides the dangers within.

Required Bosses

Uumuu

Guardian of the Teacher’s Archive in Fog Canyon. Must be defeated to awaken Monomon the Teacher.

Watcher Knights

Six ancient protectors found in the Watcher’s Spire. Defeat them to reach Lurien the Watcher.

Hollow Knight

The vessel that sealed the Infection. Defeating it leads to the Radiance fight if conditions are met.

True Final Boss

The Radiance

The true final boss and source of the Infection. A being of light sealed away within the Hollow Knight.
